Our Story

Built on belief

Lumière was founded on a simple conviction: that beautiful, purposeful work can change the way people experience the world around them.

Our
origins

It began in a small studio with two people, a single lamp, and a stubborn refusal to settle. We didn't have much, but we had a point of view — and we believed that was enough to start something worth building.

"The details are not the details. They make the design."
— Charles Eames

Over the years, we've grown — carefully, deliberately — bringing in people who share an obsession with getting things right. Not perfect for perfection's sake, but right in the way that matters: true to the work, honest to the audience, and built to endure.

Today, Lumière is a team of designers, thinkers, and makers. We work across disciplines, but we're united by a single standard: does this deserve to exist? If yes, we make it remarkable.
